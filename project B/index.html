Homesickness in University

What is homesickness?

Homesickness is a complex emotional response typically characterized by a longing or yearning for one's home environment or familiar surroundings. It can manifest as a deep sense of sadness, nostalgia, or even distress when separated from the people, places, and routines that are associated with home. Homesickness often occurs when individuals are away from home for an extended period, such as during travel, relocation, or attending school or college in a different city or country. Symptoms may vary from person to person but commonly include feelings of isolation, difficulty adjusting to new surroundings, and a desire to return to familiar comforts. Homesickness can impact mental well-being and may require coping strategies or support from loved ones to navigate effectively.
